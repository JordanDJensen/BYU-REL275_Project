SECTION 1

Lehi's Dream

  1. Start to tree but get lost.
  2. Get to the tree but chose something else
  3. Get to tree and stay
  4. Go straight to spacious building

1 Nephi 8

      In Nephi chapter 8, Nephi's father Lehi has a dream of the tree of life. Lehi wish that all would come to partake of the fruit to feel the same joy as he did. In class we learn there are four different groups of people who did partook the fruit and those who did not wanted to.

Personal Analysis

Lehi's dream is one of my favorite stories in the Book of Mormon. Before the class, my interpretation of the dream was there was only those who went to the tree and those who went to the spacious building. I never thought of the other two groups of people we discuss in class. Knowing this now, I want to do whatever it takes to help those stay on the covenant path and never leave the joy that the gospel brings.

SECTION 2

Great Apostacy

  1. Removal of "plain and precious" truth
  2. Identity of Great Abominable church
  3. Replacement or Remnant

1 Nephi 13

      In 1 Nephi chapter 13 an angel of the Lord is explaining to Nephi about the great and abominable church. During the visit Nephi learns that the church of the devil is known as the great abominable church. Also, of how the great abominable church takes away plain and precious gospel truths.

Personal Analysis

The class where we learned about the great and abominable church was one of my favorites. It was interesting to learn that the devil is the one who is actually the leader of the great and abominable church. My personal thoughts before the class was anyone who wasn't part of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints were part of the abominable church.
